Action gate (Chemical Safety Governor)

HARD holds cannot be overridden. Reaction-temperature and evidence-completeness are independently recomputed, never trusted from the proposal; an unresolved process-safety concern permanently blocks logging/release of its batch until a human-approved resolution flag clears it.

OpGate
:log-production-batchALWAYS human approval · spec-basis citation independently checked
:release-batchALWAYS human approval · evidence, reaction-temperature, product-type, safety-concern and handoff all independently re-verified
:flag-safety-concernALWAYS human approval (even at low severity) · required fields checked
:schedule-maintenanceauto-commit when clean, no capital/product risk
